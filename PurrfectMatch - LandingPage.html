About Us

Established in 2020; our professionals here at Purrfect Match strive to help match you with your perfect feline friend! Using our algorithm, we ensure that you find your Purrfect Match.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that our cats are adopted by a loving and caring person(s) and develop a close relationship with their new friend. While we cannot ensure that all animals find thier new home, we strive to make that dream as close to a reality by actively marketing, caring for, and ensuring that our animals are taken care of.

Our Cats

All of our cats go through a rigourous medical examination, are vaccinated, neutered/spayed, and go through a personality test. Additionally, we also provide all of our cats with tracking chips in the event they are lost. Finally, we provide a bag of food and toys so that you can focus on what is actually important; caring for your new friend!
