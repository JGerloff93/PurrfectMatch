About Our Application!

Unfortunately, many of our animals are castaways, rehoused, or victims of animal abuse. As a result, we take our vetting services very seriously. All of our cats are spayed/neutered, vaccinated, and chipped. If you are looking to adopt, please fill out the below form and ensure that you meet the requirements below:

  • At least 16 years of age.
    • Or a co-appliant who is.
  • Have the resources to take care of a cat.
  • Can dedicate at least an hour a day to your cat.
